Arata Garden Residences

Location: Phnom Penh

Year: 2019

ARATA Garden Residences is located in a rapidly growing area of Phnom Penh, near Aeon Mall 2 and Makro. A joint project by Creed, Triple Gem, and Japan’s Hoosiers Holdings, ARATA blends Japanese design, construction quality, and management standards. The project offers 737 homes including Shop Houses, Link Villas, Queen Villas, and King Villas. Designed by architect Hisaya Sugiyama, ARATA features a unique 1-hectare park with shaded walking trails, creating a vibrant community space for residents to relax and connect.

Living Room – Arata Garden Residences

The living spaces at ARATA Garden Residences reflect a perfect harmony of modern Japanese-inspired design and natural warmth. This living room showcases a clean, minimalist aesthetic enhanced by warm wood accents, open shelving, and greenery—bringing the outdoors in. Soft lighting and neutral tones create a calm, inviting atmosphere, while the open layout encourages easy flow between spaces. With thoughtful touches like the vertical garden feature, stylish built-ins, and sleek furniture, the ARATA living room is crafted for both comfort and refined living.

Arata's Pools

At ARATA Garden Residences, the pool is more than just a leisure amenity—it’s a tranquil escape. Designed with a focus on relaxation and wellness, the pool area is surrounded by thoughtfully landscaped greenery and offers residents a private, refreshing retreat within the community. Whether it’s a morning swim or a quiet evening by the water, the serene atmosphere reflects ARATA’s commitment to harmony, comfort, and the refined simplicity of Japanese living. embody a perfect fusion of wellness and luxury.
