Bodaiju Residences

Location: Phnom Penh

Year: 2018

A symbol of architectural finesse, the six towers of Bodaiju rise proudly along Russian Boulevard, showcasing a harmonious balance of Japanese design principles and modern urban aesthetics. The buildings are intentionally spaced to prioritize privacy while still fostering community. Clad in clean lines and timeless finishes, each structure reflects the developer’s commitment to quality and precision. This is not just real estate — it’s a statement of refined living in Phnom Penh’s most promising residential corridor.

Living Room – Bodaiju Residences

Bodaiju’s interiors are thoughtfully designed for both comfort and style. The living area features open layouts, warm finishes, and large windows that invite natural light and city views. The bedrooms offer a quiet retreat with soft lighting, built-in storage, and a calming atmosphere — perfect for rest and relaxation in the heart of the city.

Sky Pools

Perched high above the bustling city, Bodaiju’s sky pools offer residents a serene oasis in the sky. Framed by lush greenery and expansive views, the pools are more than a place to unwind — they are an experience. Whether you're enjoying a morning swim or a sunset soak, the calming ambiance of water paired with architectural elegance makes every moment feel like a retreat. With carefully designed lounging areas and shaded relaxation spaces, the pool decks embody a perfect fusion of wellness and luxury.
