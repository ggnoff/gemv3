Borey Moha Sensok

Location: Phnom Penh

Year: 2019

Borey Maha Sen Sok, developed by Japan’s Creed Group, offers thoughtfully planned homes in one of Phnom Penh’s fastest-growing districts. Spanning over 114,000 sqm, the project features 696 units, including flat houses and land for villa construction, ranging from 57 to 505 sqm. Completed in 2017, this community is just 10 minutes from AEON Mall II, ensuring easy access to shopping, dining, and leisure. With open green spaces, a playground, and an outdoor gym, Borey Maha Sen Sok combines affordability with quality, making it ideal for both homeowners and investors in Cambodia.

Kitchen – Borey Moha Sensok

The kitchens and bedrooms blend luxury with everyday functionality. Kitchens boast sleek, efficient layouts, generous storage, and premium, easy-care surfaces, perfect for both family meals and entertaining. Bedrooms provide elegant, serene retreats with plush finishes and smart storage, creating a sophisticated yet comfortable living space. Together, they embody modern luxury designed for effortless living.

Playground

The real estate also features a charming small playground designed with children in mind. It offers a safe, fun space where kids can play, explore, and socialize close to home—adding a family-friendly touch that complements the luxury and comfort of the living spaces.
